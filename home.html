Home

Welcome! We’re glad to share Seeds of Solidarity Farm and Education Center programs, resources, and inspiration with you.

We strive to support and unite people diverse in culture, race, life experience, and gender identity in all of our programs.

Our self-serve farmstand at 165 Chestnut Hill Road, Orange, MA is open mid-April to November, daylight hours, featuring fresh salad and cooking greens and more.

Our 2025 Schedule of farming/gardening and building workshops, our reconnect retreat, a new garden altar making workshop, and other events is here! Workshops begin in April, some free or sliding scale, some with full scholarships. Registration is open now!
