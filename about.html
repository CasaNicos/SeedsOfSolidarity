About

Seeds of Solidarity consists of two entities that are fiscally distinct, but connected by land, spirit and vision: Seeds of Solidarity Farm, and Seeds of Solidarity Education Center Inc, a 501 c 3 non-profit organization with a passionate staff of 4, a sage board of directors, and wonderful community partnerships.

Our Values

Education

Hands-on learning that helps anyone become a producer, not just a consumer.

Deb and Rick — Seeds of Solidarity was founded by Ricky Baruc and Deb Habib, who met in 1984 while working at the New Alchemy Institute on Cape Cod, MA — a project focused on sustainable, human-scale farming. Inspired by that experience, they spent the next decade farming and teaching environmental education, later joining the 1994–95 I nternational Pilgrimage for Peace and Life from Auschwitz to Hiroshima. In 1996, they founded Seeds of Solidarity Farm and Education Center in that same spirit of peace, sustainability, and community.

Our Home — Located on a 30-acre site in Orange, Massachusetts, Seeds of Solidarity transforms once-abandoned land into a thriving farm and learning center. Through community partnerships, education programs, and local events, we help revitalize the North Quabbin region once impacted by lost farmland and mill closures.
